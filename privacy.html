Privacy Statement


Your Privacy Matters to Us

At our BnB, we collect some personal information to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. This includes details like your:
  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Interactions with us

Why We Collect Your Information

We gather this information to better understand your needs and provide you with top-notch service. Specifically, we use your personal information to:
  • Fulfill reservations
  • Respond to your queries
  • Provide quotes, invoices, and order confirmations

Your Choice, Your Control

Providing your contact details is optional. However, if you choose not to share this information, we won't be able to send you updates or notifications about your bookings.

Keeping Your Information Safe

We take your privacy seriously. Your information is stored in encrypted files, and only certain staff members have access to it. We keep your information for seven years, after which we securely erase all associated data.
